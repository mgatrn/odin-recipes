Tiramisu

This no bake dessert comes together quickly and is great to accompany any Italian meal.

Ingredients

  • 3 large eggs
  • 1/2 cup espresso or strong coffee, cooled
  • 1/2 cup sugar
  • 2 tablespoons cognac or brandy (optional)
  • 8 oz. mascarpone
  • 2 tablespoons cocoa
  • 10 ladyfingers

Instructions
  1. Step 1 - Combine 3 egg yolks, 1 tablespoon espresso, sugar and cognac into a large bowl. Beat 2-3 minutes. Add mascarpone and beat 3-5 minutes until smooth.
  2. Step 2 - In another bowl, combine 3 egg whites and a pinch of sugar. Beat until stiff peaks form. Gently fold into mascarpone mixture.
  3. Step 3 - Quickly dip each ladyfinger into remaining espresso and layer on bottom of small serving dish. Spread mascarpone mixture and sprinkle with cocoa. Regrigerate at least 1 hour before serving.
