Salad

This simple Italian salad is the only side salad recipe you need. It fits perfectly with any pasta dish!

Ingredients

  • 1 head leafy romaine lettuce, roughly chopped
  • 1/2 medium red onion, thinly sliced
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1 cup peperoncini, whole or sliced
  • 1/2 cup black olives
  • 1 pint cherry tomatoes
  • Freshly ground pepper, to taste
  • Croutons, store bought or homemade

Italian Dressing

  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon sea salt
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 2 tablespoons red wine vinegar

Instructions
  1. Step 1 - Add all of the dressing ingredients to a tightly fitted jar. Shake vigorously until all ingredients are combined. Store in refrigerator for a week. Shake well before use.
  2. Step 2 - Add all salad ingredients to a large salad bowl. Drizzle with Italian dressing and toss to combine.
