Lasagna

It's easy, it's cheesy, and it's vegetarian!

Ingredients

  • 9 lasagna noodles
  • 1 bunch of fresh spinach
  • 8 ounces ricotta cheese
  • 1/2 cup shredded mozzarella cheese
  • 1 egg, lightly beaten
  • 1 pinch ground nutmeg
  • 1 pinch dried basil
  • 1 cup pasta sauce
  • 1/4 cup grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions
  1. Step 1 - Preheat oven to 400 degrees Farenheit. Grease a cassarole dish with cookin spray.
  2. Step 2 - Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook lasagna in the boiling water according to the package directions. When cooked, drain the pasta water.
  3. Step 3 - Place a steamer insert into a saucepan and fill with water to just below the bottom of the steamer. Bring water to a boil. Add spinach, cover, and steam until tender, 2 to 6 minutes. Drain spinach.
  4. Step 4 - Mix spinach, ricotta cheese, 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese, egg, nutmeg, basil, salt and pepper in a bowl until thoroughlu combined.
  5. Step 5 - Spread 1/4 cup pasta sauce on the bottom of the prepared casserole dish. Top with 3 lasagna noodles, 1/2 of the ricotta mixture, and 1/4 cut pasta sauce. Repeat layers of 3 more noodles, 1/2 cup ricotta mixture, and 1/4 cut pasta sauce. End with the remaining 3 lasagna noodles and 1/4 cut pasta sauce. Sprinkle 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese and Parmesan cheese on top. Cover casserole with aluminum foil.
  6. Step 6 - Bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes. Uncover casserole and continue bakint until lasagna is bubbling and lightly browned, about 25 more minutes. Let lasagna stand 5 minutes before serving
