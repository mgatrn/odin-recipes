Garlic Bread

This Italian Garlic Bread is an easy homemade recipe made with a garlic and herb butter and Parmesan cheese. It comes together in about 15 minutes and is crispy, buttery and garlicky! It's a perfect side to serve with pastas, soups, stews, and salads.

Ingredients

  • Italian or French loaf or 1 large baguette
  • Unsalted butter
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • 3-4 cloves of garlic, finely minced
  • 1-2 teaspoons dried italian herbs
  • Grated Parmesan cheese

Instructions
  1. Preheat oven to 400 degrees Farenheit.
  2. Prepare Cookie Sheet - Line a cookie sheet with parchment paper. Set aside
  3. Slice Bread - Slice the baguette in half lengthwise and then cut each half into three pieces. Place the cut pieces of bread on the prepared cookie sheet.
  4. Make Garlic and Herb Butter - Set the butter out to soften at room temperature. Blend the olive oil and minced garlic together in a blender until the garlic is mostly blended into the oil and what's left are very fine pieces of garlic. Mix together the softened butter and garlic oil in a small bowl. Add dried herbs and grated Parmesean cheese to taste.
  5. Butter the Bread - Generously spead the garlic and herb butter on the sliced pieces of baguette. Optional - sprinkle the top with Parmesean cheese.
  6. Bake - Bake for 10-12 minutes.
